The High Cost of Living in the US

By Danny Chung-A-Fung

April 20, 2026

Housing prices have gone up a whole lot in recent years, which makes it harder for Americans to find a place to live and raise a family. This is true in Maryland, where in the more expensive counties, the average median home price is as much as $650,000.

One factor that contributes to high housing prices is interest rates, which has also increased a lot over the last 10 years.
